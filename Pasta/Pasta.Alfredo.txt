Pasta - (Penne/Fussili/whatever)
Butter - 50-60g
Parmesan - as much as you want.

Alternative extras:
Peas, onion, garlic, parsley, etc
Bacon, Shrimp, Pancetta, Chorizo, etc
Black Pepper, Cayenne Powder, etc

1) Cook Pasta in a pan until toothy/al dente
2) Reserve some pasta water, leave some in pot.
3) Add butter, stir unti emulsion has formed.
4) add parmesan, stir until creamy
5) Add any other ingredients, and keep stirring.
6) Add any desired seasoning, black pepper\salt\cayenne powder\etc
7) Once everything is hot/warm remove from heat, put into bowls, eat
