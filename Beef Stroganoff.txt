2 Tbsp. of olive oil
250g mushrooms
1 large onion
1 garlic, chopped
500g Beef, minced
4 tsp paprika
0.5 litre beef stock
As much Egg noodles/Pasta as you feel like
200ml sour cream
1 Tbsp cornstarch slurry
curly leaf parsley for garnish
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:

Pre-Prep:
Dice Onion
Dice Garlic
Prepare Water for stock

1 - In a large pot, heat olive oil.
2 - Add mushrooms and season with salt and pepper.
3 - Add the onions and garlic. Cook until onions are translucent. Medium high heat.
4 - Raise temperature and add the beef & half the paprika, cook until browned.
5 - Stir in the rest of the paprika.
6 - Add beef stock and noodles/pasta.
7 - Give it a stir and cover.
8 - Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
9 - Lower the heat, add the sour cream and cornstarch slurry. Stir until thickened.

Eat and Enjoy
