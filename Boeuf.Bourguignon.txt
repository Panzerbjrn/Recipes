Boeuf Bourguignon

Ingredients:
Onions
Stock
1 bottle of Burgundy
Garlic
Beef
Bay Leaves
Carrots

Prep:
Salt & Pepper the beef
Cut onions
Peel Carrots
Cut Bacon.
Heat oven to Max
Prepare Stock

1) Brown the beef so it is well crusty.
2) Add onions and carrots to the pan.
3) Add beef to Dutch oven, add flour, mix well. Put in oven uncovered for 10 minutes, turn, return to oven for 10 minutes.
4) Remove onions and carrots from thee pan. Add wine, scrub off the fond.
5) Put everything in the Dutch oven, cook in oven for 3 hours stirring occasionally.
