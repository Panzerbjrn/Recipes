Ranch Dressing: https://youtu.be/mh2AXh1eRmE?t=370


For each 0.5kg of chicken, add 1tsp of:	
-Coarse Salt
-Baking Powder
-Corn Starch
Toss and mix on chicken pieces

Place in fridge for 3-24 hours
Oven is 230c/450f for 20 minutes Skin up.
Check? Leave up to 10 minutes extra.
Flip wings, Give them another 15ish minutes.

Toss in whatever sauce you like.
