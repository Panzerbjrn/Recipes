Bread

Babish's No knead bread

400g Bread Flour
10g salt
1g 1/4 tsp yeast
266-333ml water, but probably:
--314ml water Too much
--260ml water Fie


1) Mix flour / yeast /salt - Whisk gently
2) Add water, stir with spatula until even
3) leave 12-24 hours - Until lacy, with bubble and smelling of fermentation.
4) Pull corners towards the centre, then  pull bottom in towards itself to make a dome.
5) Restunder tea towel for 1-2 hours or until grown by 50%
6) Heat oven to as hot as it will go with the cast iron dutch oven inside
7) Score the bread, drop into Dutch Oven, spray with water, bake for 30 minutes
8) @15 minutes in, remove lid, spritz with more water
9) Leave to rest for two hours

Egg Wash - 1 whole egg + splash of whole milk

Tangzhong mix:
1) Mix whole milk / Bread Flour / Water in small pot.
2) Which over medium heat. takes about 30 second to become gelatinous. Remove, put in bowl or whatever

Milk & Yeast mix
3) 120g whole milk heat to 90f/35c
4) Add 9g instant yeast, stir 
5) Leave for 8 minutes

Combine:
6) Stand mixer bowl: 320g Bread flour, 35g Sugar, 7g fine sea salt, whisk to get mixed.
7) Add dough hook, mix on low speed, add milk & yeast mix
8) Leave to run for a few seconds, then add Tangzhong mix
9) Add 1 whole egg + 1 egg yolk
10) Increase to medium low, and mix until thorooughly incorporated.
11) Gradually add 42g soft unsalted butter
12) leave to knead for up to 10 minutes, or until nice and smooth

13) Grease a bowl, make the dough into a ball (Rotate towards you, see video 3:41 for technique)
14) Place in bowl, cover with damp towel, place in warm area for 1-1.5 hours. or until doubled in size.
15) Punch the dough
16) Divide dough into even pieces, bout 90-100g
17) Shape into little doughy balls, Similar to step 13.
18) Place on baking paper on ightly greased baking paper on rimmed baking sheet, about 2.5" apart
19) Place Baking sheet inverted on top and leave to rise at room temp for 1-2 hours. Or until doubled.
20) Wash with egg wash.
21) Bake at 375f/190c for 16-18 minutes, or a deep golden brown.
22) Remove, brush immediately with melted butter or browned butter if you're fancy.
