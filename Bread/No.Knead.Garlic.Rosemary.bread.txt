Ingredients:

2 cups Warm Water
2 tsp Instant Yeast
2 tsp Salt
3 cups Flour
1 head of Garlic (yes really)
1 tbsp Dried Rosemary


1) In a large bowl, add yeast and warm water, leave to rest for a bit until bloomed.
2) Add Flour, Salt Garlic, and Rosemary to the bowl with the Water and Yeast.
3) Use spatula to fold the dough until itâ€™s mixed and a sticky dough forms. 
4) Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let rest for 30 minutes
5) Preheat oven to 450f
6) Once the dough has doubled in size, shape into a ball.
7) Place an oven safe container of water on the lower rack of your oven. Then place dough ball on baking sheet and place the baking sheet in the oven on the top rack.
8) Bake for 30 minutes. Remove container of water. Continue to bake for 15 minutes.

Optional: Before baking coat the ball of dough with olive oil.
