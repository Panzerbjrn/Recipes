Ingredients
Pickled onions
Red onions - sliced thinly root to stem
1 part water
1 part vinegar
Big pinch of salt
Big pinch of sugar

To make:
1) Add the sliced red onion to the final jar you will keep them in.
2) Fill the container half way with water and half way with vinegar.
3) Add in a large pinch of salt and sugar.
4) Heat a saucepan over medium heat.
5) Add the onion mixture and bring to a simmer.
6) Turn off the heat and add the onions back to the jar.
7) Then pour the liquids into the jar.

They will store in the fridge for several weeks.
