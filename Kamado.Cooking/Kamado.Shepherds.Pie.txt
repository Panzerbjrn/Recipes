Ingredients
1.5kg Lamb/chuck Beef
Oniion Grated
Carrots Grated
Rosemary
Thyme
Guiness



Smoking:
1) 250f for about 1.25-1.5 hours
2) Double Wrap in tinfoil for another 30-45 minutes at 350f. Should be 200-215f intenally.




Gravy:
1) Add to a hot skillet: Oniion, Carrots, Rosemary, Thyme To,ato paste
2) Incorporate everything
3) add flour
4) when thickened add 1 cup beef stock
5) When thickened, add guiness
6) Simmer and thicken
7) Add meat and peas


Pie:
1) Increase to 400-425f
2) 45 minutes, then close cap
3) Wait until browned.



170f - 75c
