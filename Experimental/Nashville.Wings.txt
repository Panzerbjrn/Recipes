Nashville Hot Wings

Tasty: https://youtu.be/iummgPuiGVk?t=105
Cayenne 1/2 cup
Garlic, Granulated 6tbsp
Black Pepper 2tbsp
Salt 2tbsp
Paprika 6tbsp
Onion, Granulated 6tbsp
Brown Sugar, 4tbsp

Chicken 3lbs
Butter Milk 4 cups

Deep fry at 325f/163c 8-10 minutes.

3/4 cup lard. melt in pot
Add last of mixture
toss with chicken
