Creamy Bourbon Mushroom Sauce
Source: Jim Beam Cookbook p59

4 shallots
600g mushrooms
100ml Jim Beam Bourbon
100ml Double Cream

If cooking steak/chops, use the same pan.

1) Heat pan to medium, add shallots, fry for a minute.
2) Add mushrooms, stir and coat them in the fat rendered from cooked meat or oil
3) Season with sal & pepper, stir, add bourbon.
4) Cook until meat has rested, or 5-10 minutes.
5) Reduce heat, stir in cream.
6) Serve
