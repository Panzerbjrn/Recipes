Buffalo Hot Sauce:
1:1 Butter:Hot Sauce

Creamy Buffalo Hot Sauce:
.5:.5:1 Plain Greek Yogurt:Butter:Hot Sauce
or:
.5:.5:1 Mayo:Butter:Hot Sauce

1) Heat butter. When bubbling, add hot sauce
2) Stir to create emulsion
3) When firm and thickened a bit, remove from heat.
4) Leave to cool for a couple of minutes.
5) Add yogurt/Mayo and stir to combine.
6) Add optionals
7) Taste for flavour
8) Add to bottle, store in fridge for 2+ weeks.
