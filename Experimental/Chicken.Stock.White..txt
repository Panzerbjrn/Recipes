White Chicken Stock

Sources:
JW: https://youtu.be/rjDHii3Ngj8

Add to pot
Bring to rapid boil
leave for 10 second.
strain, rinse bones/meat.

Add to clean pot

Add celery, onion, parsnip, garlic, white mushrooms, leek
Add bay leaf
Add water to cover an inch or two above the bones
Heat to boil, then reduce to a simmer
lightly Simmer 3-4 hours
Remove skum intermittently
Remove bones & Strain. 
Voila
