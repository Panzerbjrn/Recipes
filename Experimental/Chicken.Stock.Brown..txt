3Brown Chicken Stock

Sources:
JW: https://youtu.be/rjDHii3Ngj8

Add any herbs desired...

1) Put on baking rack, drizzle with oil.
420f 218c @ 40-45 minutes
Add to pot

Add 1 carrot, 2 celery stalks diced roughly 
Add 1 onion cut into 8ths
Add water to cover an incoh above the bones
Heat to boil, then reduce to a simmer
Remove skum intermittently
Simmer 2-4 (ideally 3 hours at least)
Remove bones & Strain. 
Voila
