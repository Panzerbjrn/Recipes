Garlic Parmesan Wings

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D_YGIeVM3FE

Chicken
Salt
Pepper
Italian Seasoning 1tbsp
Cumin 1tsp
Mix and toss well

Bake at 300f/150c for 20 minutes
Increase to 425f/220c for 15 minutes

Parmesan Cheese 1/3cup
Minced garlic 5 cloves
Olive oil 1/4cup
Fresh Parsley 1/4cup
Mix well

Add chicken.
Mix well, serve.
