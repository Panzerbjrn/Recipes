Ingredients:
500g Beef
1 Onion
Garlic
Cumin Seeds
Chilli Powder
Worcestershire sauce
Tomato Pureé
can of chopped tomatoes
Oregano
Sugar

Steps:
1) Gently fry onions until soft
2) Add Garlic & Cumin Seeds. leave until fragrant.
3) Add chili powder
4) Turn the heat up up up, add Beef, make sure it browns *not^ steams.
5) Add Worcestershire sauce, let it incorporate, add Tomato Pureé, let it cook through.
6) Add Tinned tomatoes, stir, Oregano, stir, sugar, stir.
7) Leave to reduce to desired consistency
